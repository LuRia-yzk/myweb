红色故事

经典革命故事英文读本
Classic Revolutionary Stories in English

长征故事 The Long March

1934 年至 1936 年，中国工农红军进行了举世闻名的二万五千里长征。红军爬雪山、过草地，克服了无数艰难险阻，最终到达陕北，保存了革命火种。

From 1934 to 1936, the Chinese Workers' and Peasants' Red Army carried out the world-famous 25,000-li Long March. They climbed snow-capped mountains, crossed grasslands, overcame countless difficulties, and finally reached northern Shaanxi, preserving the revolutionary flame.

井冈山会师 The Jinggangshan Meeting

1928 年 4 月，毛泽东率领的秋收起义部队与朱德、陈毅领导的南昌起义部队在井冈山胜利会师，创建了中国第一个农村革命根据地。

In April 1928, the Autumn Harvest Uprising forces led by Mao Zedong met with the Nanchang Uprising forces led by Zhu De and Chen Yi at Jinggangshan, establishing China's first rural revolutionary base area.

飞夺泸定桥 Seizing Luding Bridge

1935 年 5 月，红军勇士冒着枪林弹雨，攀踏着铁索桥，成功夺取泸定桥，为红军主力开辟了前进道路，展现了大无畏的革命英雄主义精神。

In May 1935, Red Army soldiers braved heavy fire, climbed on iron-chain bridge planks, and successfully captured Luding Bridge, opening the way for the main force and demonstrating fearless revolutionary heroism.

半条棉被 Half a Quilt

长征途中，三位女红军借宿在湖南汝城县沙洲村徐解秀老人家中，临走时把自己仅有的一床被子剪下一半留给老人。这就是"半条棉被"的故事，体现了军民鱼水情。

During the Long March, three female Red Army soldiers stayed at Xu Jiexiu's home in Shazhou Village. When leaving, they cut their only quilt in half and left it to the old woman. This story of "half a quilt" embodies the close bond between the army and the people.

革命精神

伟大精神的英文解读与传承
English Interpretation of Great Revolutionary Spirit

长征精神 The Long March Spirit

把全国人民和中华民族的根本利益看得高于一切，坚定革命的理想和信念，坚信正义事业必然胜利的精神；为了救国救民，不怕任何艰难险阻，不惜付出一切牺牲的精神。

Putting the fundamental interests of the Chinese people and nation above all else; firm revolutionary ideals and beliefs; conviction that justice will triumph; fearlessness of any difficulty or sacrifice for the salvation of the country and people.

抗战精神 The War of Resistance Spirit

天下兴亡、匹夫有责的爱国情怀；视死如归、宁死不屈的民族气节；不畏强暴、血战到底的英雄气概；百折不挠、坚忍不拔的必胜信念。

Patriotic spirit of "everyone is responsible for the fate of the nation"; national integrity of facing death unflinchingly; heroic courage of fighting against tyranny; unwavering determination and perseverance for ultimate victory.

延安精神 The Yan'an Spirit

坚定正确的政治方向，解放思想、实事求是的思想路线，全心全意为人民服务的根本宗旨，自力更生、艰苦奋斗的创业精神。

Firm and correct political direction; ideological line of emancipating the mind and seeking truth from facts; fundamental purpose of serving the people wholeheartedly; pioneering spirit of self-reliance and hard struggle.

西柏坡精神 The Xibaipo Spirit

谦虚谨慎、艰苦奋斗的精神；敢于斗争、敢于胜利的精神；依靠群众、团结统一的精神。这是中国革命转折时期的精神财富。

Spirit of modesty, prudence, and hard struggle; courage to fight and win; spirit of relying on the masses and maintaining unity. This is the spiritual treasure during the turning point of the Chinese revolution.

英模人物

革命先烈与时代楷模英文介绍
English Introductions to Revolutionary Martyrs and Models

方志敏 Fang Zhimin

方志敏（1899-1935），江西弋阳人，无产阶级革命家、军事家。他在狱中写下《可爱的中国》等不朽篇章，表达了对祖国深沉的爱和对革命必胜的信念。

Fang Zhimin (1899-1935), from Yiyang, Jiangxi, was a proletarian revolutionary and strategist. In prison, he wrote immortal works like "Lovely China," expressing deep love for his motherland and firm belief in revolutionary victory.

赵一曼 Zhao Yiman

赵一曼（1905-1936），四川宜宾人，抗日民族英雄。她在东北抗日联军中英勇作战，被捕后坚贞不屈，壮烈牺牲，年仅 31 岁。她的英雄事迹激励了一代又一代中国人。

Zhao Yiman (1905-1936), from Yibin, Sichuan, was an anti-Japanese national hero. She fought bravely in the Northeast Anti-Japanese United Army, remained steadfast after capture, and sacrificed heroically at age 31. Her deeds inspire generations of Chinese people.

董存瑞 Dong Cunrui

董存瑞（1929-1948），河北怀来人，全国著名战斗英雄。1948 年在解放隆化的战斗中，他手托炸药包炸毁敌人碉堡，用生命为部队开辟了前进道路。

Dong Cunrui (1929-1948), from Huailai, Hebei, was a nationally renowned combat hero. In 1948, during the battle to liberate Longhua, he held an explosive pack and destroyed an enemy bunker, sacrificing his life to open the way for his troops.

黄继光 Huang Jiguang

黄继光（1931-1952），四川中江人，中国人民志愿军特级战斗英雄。在抗美援朝上甘岭战役中，他用胸膛堵住敌人机枪射孔，壮烈牺牲，年仅 21 岁。

Huang Jiguang (1931-1952), from Zhongjiang, Sichuan, was a special-class combat hero of the Chinese People's Volunteers. During the Battle of Shangganling in the War to Resist US Aggression and Aid Korea, he blocked enemy machine gun fire with his chest, sacrificing heroically at age 21.

雷锋 Lei Feng

雷锋（1940-1962），湖南长沙人，伟大的共产主义战士。他以无私奉献、助人为乐的精神闻名，成为几代中国人的学习榜样。"雷锋精神"已成为中华民族宝贵的精神财富。

Lei Feng (1940-1962), from Changsha, Hunan, was a great communist fighter. He became famous for his spirit of selfless dedication and helping others, becoming a role model for generations of Chinese people. "Lei Feng Spirit" has become a precious spiritual asset of the Chinese nation.

焦裕禄 Jiao Yulu

焦裕禄（1922-1964），山东淄博人，县委书记的榜样。他在河南兰考县工作期间，带领群众治理风沙、内涝、盐碱"三害"，被誉为"党的好干部"、"人民的好公仆"。

Jiao Yulu (1922-1964), from Zibo, Shandong, was a model county party secretary. While working in Lankao County, Henan, he led the masses in controlling wind, waterlogging, and salinization. He was hailed as "the Party's good cadre" and "the people's good servant."

红色基地

全国红色教育基地英文讲解资料
English Introduction to National Red Education Bases

井冈山 Jinggangshan

位于江西省西南部，是中国革命的摇篮。1927 年，毛泽东在这里创建了中国第一个农村革命根据地，开辟了"农村包围城市、武装夺取政权"的革命道路。

Located in southwestern Jiangxi Province, Jinggangshan is the cradle of the Chinese revolution. In 1927, Mao Zedong established China's first rural revolutionary base area here, opening up the revolutionary path of "encircling cities from rural areas and seizing political power through armed struggle."

延安 Yan'an

位于陕西省北部，是中国革命的圣地。1935 年至 1948 年，中共中央在这里领导中国革命 13 年，培育了光照千秋的延安精神。

Located in northern Shaanxi Province, Yan'an is a holy land of the Chinese revolution. From 1935 to 1948, the CPC Central Committee led the Chinese revolution from here for 13 years, nurturing the radiant Yan'an Spirit.

遵义会议旧址 The Zunyi Conference Site

位于贵州省遵义市，1935 年 1 月，中共中央政治局在这里召开扩大会议，确立了毛泽东在党和红军中的领导地位，是党的历史上生死攸关的转折点。

Located in Zunyi City, Guizhou Province. In January 1935, the Political Bureau of the CPC Central Committee held an enlarged meeting here, establishing Mao Zedong's leadership position in the Party and the Red Army. It was a life-and-death turning point in the Party's history.

西柏坡 Xibaipo

位于河北省平山县，是解放战争时期中共中央所在地。在这里，党中央和毛泽东指挥了三大战役，召开了具有伟大历史意义的七届二中全会。

Located in Pingshan County, Hebei Province, Xibaipo was where the CPC Central Committee was stationed during the War of Liberation. From here, the Party Central Committee and Mao Zedong commanded three major campaigns and convened the historic Second Plenary Session of the Seventh Central Committee.

卢沟桥 Lugou Bridge

位于北京市丰台区，1937 年 7 月 7 日，日本侵略者在这里发动了震惊中外的"七七事变"，标志着中国全民族抗战的开始。

Located in Fengtai District, Beijing. On July 7, 1937, Japanese aggressors launched the shocking "July 7th Incident" here, marking the beginning of China's nationwide war of resistance against Japanese aggression.
