传统节日

中国传统节日的英文介绍与习俗讲解
Traditional Chinese Festivals and Customs

春节 Spring Festival

春节是中国最重要的传统节日，象征着团圆和新的开始。人们会贴春联、放鞭炮、吃年夜饭、给压岁钱。

The Spring Festival is the most important traditional festival in China, symbolizing reunion and new beginnings. People paste spring couplets, set off firecrackers, have reunion dinners, and give red envelopes.

端午节 Dragon Boat Festival

端午节纪念爱国诗人屈原，主要习俗包括赛龙舟、吃粽子、挂艾草，体现了中华民族的爱国情怀。

The Dragon Boat Festival commemorates the patriotic poet Qu Yuan. Main customs include dragon boat racing, eating zongzi, and hanging mugwort, reflecting the patriotism of the Chinese nation.

中秋节 Mid-Autumn Festival

中秋节是团圆的节日，人们赏月、吃月饼、饮桂花酒，寄托对家人团聚的美好愿望。

The Mid-Autumn Festival is a festival of reunion. People admire the full moon, eat mooncakes, and drink osmanthus wine, expressing good wishes for family reunion.

重阳节 Double Ninth Festival

重阳节有登高、赏菊、插茱萸等习俗，现在也被定为老人节，倡导尊老敬老的传统美德。

The Double Ninth Festival features customs such as climbing mountains, admiring chrysanthemums, and wearing dogwood. It is now also designated as Elderly Day, promoting the virtue of respecting the elderly.

经典诗词

唐诗宋词英译欣赏与诵读
English Translation and Recitation of Tang Poetry and Song Ci

静夜思 Li Bai

床前明月光，
疑是地上霜。
举头望明月，
低头思故乡。

In the Still Night by Li Bai:
Bright moonlight before my bed,
Suspected frost upon the ground.
I raise my head to watch the bright moon,
I lower my head and think of homeland.

春晓 Meng Haoran

春眠不觉晓，
处处闻啼鸟。
夜来风雨声，
花落知多少。

Spring Dawn by Meng Haoran:
Sleeping in spring, I know not dawn,
Everywhere I hear birds singing.
Last night came sounds of wind and rain,
How many flowers have fallen?

水调歌头 Su Shi

明月几时有？
把酒问青天。
不知天上宫阙，
今夕是何年。

Shuidiao Getou by Su Shi:
When will the moon be clear and bright?
With wine in hand, I ask the sky.
I wonder what time it is tonight,
In those celestial courts on high.

非遗技艺

中国非物质文化遗产的英文讲解
English Introduction to Intangible Cultural Heritage

剪纸 Paper Cutting

剪纸是中国传统民间艺术，用剪刀或刻刀在纸上创作图案，常用于装饰和节日庆典，表达美好祝愿。

Paper cutting is a traditional Chinese folk art. Artists create patterns with scissors or carving knives on paper, often used for decoration and festivals, expressing good wishes.

书法 Calligraphy

中国书法是汉字书写艺术，通过毛笔的运笔技巧表现文字的美感和神韵，是中华文化的重要瑰宝。

Chinese calligraphy is the art of writing Chinese characters. It expresses the beauty and spirit of characters through brush techniques, representing a precious treasure of Chinese culture.

戏曲 Chinese Opera

中国戏曲是综合性表演艺术，融合唱、念、做、打四种艺术手法，京剧被誉为"国粹"。

Chinese opera is a comprehensive performing art that integrates singing, recitation, acting, and acrobatics. Peking Opera is known as the "quintessence" of Chinese culture.

茶艺 Tea Art

中国茶文化源远流长，茶艺包括选茶、择水、烹煮、品饮等环节，体现了中国人的生活美学。

Chinese tea culture has a long history. Tea art includes selecting tea, choosing water, brewing, and tasting, reflecting the life aesthetics of Chinese people.

武术 Martial Arts

中国武术是传统体育项目，讲究内外兼修，既有攻防技击价值，也有健身养生功能。

Chinese martial arts are traditional sports emphasizing both internal and external cultivation. They have combat value and health-preserving functions.

中华美德

中华传统美德的德育英文素材
Moral Education Materials on Traditional Chinese Virtues

孝 Filial Piety

"百善孝为先"，孝道是中华民族的传统美德，要求子女尊敬、赡养父母，感恩养育之恩。

"Filial piety is the first among all virtues." It requires children to respect and support their parents and be grateful for their upbringing.

礼 Propriety

礼是行为规范，包括礼貌、礼仪、礼节。孔子曰："不学礼，无以立"，强调礼的重要性。

Propriety refers to behavioral norms, including politeness, etiquette, and rituals. Confucius said, "Without learning propriety, one cannot establish oneself."

诚 Honesty

诚信是做人之本，"言必信，行必果"。诚实守信用是人际交往的基本准则。

Honesty is the foundation of being a person. "Words must be trustworthy, actions must be fruitful." Being honest and keeping promises are basic principles of interpersonal communication.

和 Harmony

"和为贵"，和谐是中华文化的核心理念，追求人与人、人与自然、人与社会的和谐共处。

"Harmony is the most precious." Harmony is the core concept of Chinese culture, pursuing peaceful coexistence between people, nature, and society.

历史名人

中国古代杰出人物的英文人物介绍
English Introductions to Outstanding Historical Figures

孔子 Confucius

孔子（前 551-前 479），名丘，字仲尼，春秋时期鲁国人。他是伟大的思想家、教育家，儒家学派创始人，被尊为"至圣先师"。

Confucius (551-479 BC), named Qiu, courtesy name Zhongni, was from the State of Lu during the Spring and Autumn Period. He was a great thinker and educator, founder of the Confucian school, honored as the "Sage Teacher."

屈原 Qu Yuan

屈原（约前 340-前 278），战国时期楚国诗人、政治家。他创作了《离骚》等不朽诗篇，因忧国投江自尽，后人以端午节纪念他。

Qu Yuan (c. 340-278 BC) was a poet and statesman of the State of Chu during the Warring States Period. He created immortal poems like "Li Sao." He drowned himself out of concern for his country, and people commemorate him during the Dragon Boat Festival.

苏轼 Su Shi

苏轼（1037-1101），字子瞻，号东坡居士，北宋文学家、书画家。他的诗词文书画皆有极高成就，是唐宋八大家之一。

Su Shi (1037-1101), courtesy name Zizhan, pen name Dongpo Jushi, was a Northern Song dynasty literati, calligrapher, and painter. He achieved great success in poetry, prose, calligraphy, and painting, and is one of the Eight Great Masters of the Tang and Song dynasties.
