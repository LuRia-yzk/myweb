东坡文化 · Dongpo Culture

苏轼寓惠事迹与诗词英译

苏轼寓惠简介

Introduction to Su Shi in Huizhou

北宋文学家苏轼于绍圣元年（1094 年）被贬至惠州，在惠期间三年零九个月。他关心民生疾苦，推广农业技术，兴办教育，对惠州文化发展影响深远。

Su Shi, a renowned literary master of the Northern Song Dynasty, was exiled to Huizhou in 1094. During his three years and nine months here, he cared deeply about people's hardships, promoted agricultural techniques, and established schools, profoundly influencing Huizhou's cultural development.

东坡诗词英译

English Translation of Dongpo's Poems

《惠州一绝》

罗浮山下四时春，

卢橘杨梅次第新。

日啖荔枝三百颗，

不辞长作岭南人。

"A Poem About Huizhou"

Below Mount Luofu, spring lasts all year round,

Golden oranges and bayberries fresh in turn found.

Three hundred lychees daily I would eat,

To dwell in Lingnan forever I'd not retreat.

客家围屋建筑

Hakka Walled Village Architecture

客家围屋是客家人独特的民居建筑形式，集防御、居住、祭祀功能于一体，体现了客家人的家族观念和团结精神。

Hakka walled villages are unique residential architecture that integrates defense, living, and sacrificial functions, reflecting the family values and unity spirit of Hakka people.

东江红色文化 · Dongjiang Red Culture

东江纵队革命历史英文讲解

东江纵队简介

Introduction to Dongjiang Column

东江纵队是抗日战争时期中国共产党领导的一支重要抗日武装力量，成立于 1943 年，战斗在东江流域，为华南抗战作出了重大贡献。

The Dongjiang Column was an important anti-Japanese armed force led by the Communist Party of China during the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression. Established in 1943, it fought in the Dongjiang River basin and made significant contributions to the resistance in South China.

营救文化名人

Rescuing Cultural Celebrities

1942 年，东江纵队成功营救了数百名被困在香港的文化界人士，包括茅盾、邹韬奋等，这次秘密大营救被称为"中国文化史上的奇迹"。

In 1942, the Dongjiang Column successfully rescued hundreds of cultural figures trapped in Hong Kong, including Mao Dun and Zou Taofen. This secret rescue operation was hailed as "a miracle in Chinese cultural history."

惠州西湖风光

Huizhou West Lake Scenery

惠州西湖位于惠州市区，是中国六大西湖之一。湖面宽阔，山水相映，素有"苎萝西子"之美誉。

Huizhou West Lake, located in the urban area of Huizhou, is one of the six West Lakes in China. With its vast lake surface and reflected mountain views, it enjoys the reputation of "Zhuluo Xizi" (a beauty from ancient Yue state).

惠州城市简介

Introduction to Huizhou City

Huizhou, located in southeastern Guangdong Province, is a historic and cultural city with over 2,200 years of history. It borders Shenzhen to the south, Heyuan to the north, Shanwei to the east, and Guangzhou to the west. The city covers an area of 11,347 square kilometers and has a subtropical monsoon climate.

惠州位于广东省东南部，是一座拥有 2200 多年历史的历史文化名城。南邻深圳，北接河源，东连汕尾，西靠广州。全市面积 11347 平方公里，属亚热带季风气候。

惠州文化特色

Cultural Features of Huizhou

Huizhou boasts a rich blend of cultures: Dongpo culture represents literati traditions, Hakka culture showcases ethnic migration history, Dongjiang red culture embodies revolutionary spirit, and West Lake culture displays Lingnan landscape aesthetics. These four cultural dimensions together form Huizhou's unique cultural identity.

惠州文化多元融合：东坡文化代表文人传统，客家文化展现民族迁徙历史，东江红色文化体现革命精神，西湖文化彰显岭南山水审美。这四大文化维度共同构成了惠州独特的文化身份。
