非遗文化 · Intangible Cultural Heritage

惠州非物质文化遗产英文介绍

💡

文化小科普 | Cultural Background

惠州非遗概览：惠州拥有丰富的非物质文化遗产，包括龙门农民画、惠东渔歌、客家山歌等。这些非遗项目承载着惠州人民的历史记忆和文化基因，是岭南文化的重要组成部分。

Huizhou Heritage Overview: Huizhou boasts rich intangible cultural heritage, including Longmen Farmer Paintings, Huidong Fishing Songs, Hakka Mountain Songs, etc. These heritage items carry the historical memory and cultural genes of Huizhou people and are an important part of Lingnan culture.

龙门农民画

Longmen Farmer Paintings

龙门农民画是惠州龙门县的传统民间艺术，起源于明代，以质朴的笔触、鲜艳的色彩描绘农村生活场景，被誉为"东方民间艺术奇葩"。

Longmen Farmer Paintings are a traditional folk art in Longmen County, Huizhou, originating from the Ming Dynasty. With simple brushstrokes and bright colors, they depict rural life scenes and are praised as "a unique gem of Oriental folk art."

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于初中英语阅读课，可作为文化对比写作素材，引导学生用英语介绍中国民间艺术。

惠东渔歌

Huidong Fishing Songs

惠东渔歌是惠东沿海渔民世代传唱的传统民歌，内容涵盖捕鱼、生活、爱情等主题，旋律优美，歌词朴实，是国家级非物质文化遗产。

Huidong Fishing Songs are traditional folk songs sung by fishermen in Huidong coastal areas for generations. The songs cover themes such as fishing, life, and love, with beautiful melodies and simple lyrics. They are a national intangible cultural heritage.

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于高中英语听说课，可作为民俗文化口语表达素材，让学生学习用英语讲述中国民间音乐故事。

客家山歌

Hakka Mountain Songs

客家山歌是客家人在山区劳动生活中创作的山歌，内容广泛，形式多样，是客家文化的重要载体，表达了客家人对生活的热爱和对美好未来的向往。

Hakka Mountain Songs are folk songs created by Hakka people during their labor and life in mountainous areas. With extensive content and diverse forms, they are an important carrier of Hakka culture, expressing Hakka people's love for life and yearning for a better future.

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于小学高年级英语拓展课，可作为民族文化启蒙素材，培养学生对本土文化的兴趣和认同感。

人文景点 · Cultural Sites

惠州历史人文景点英文讲解

🏛️

文化小科普 | Cultural Background

惠州历史沿革：惠州是国家历史文化名城，已有 1400 多年历史。自古以来就是东江流域的政治、经济、文化中心，素有"岭东雄郡"之称。苏东坡、文天祥等历史名人都曾在此留下足迹。

Huizhou History: Huizhou is a national historical and cultural city with over 1,400 years of history. It has been the political, economic, and cultural center of the Dongjiang River basin since ancient times, known as "the eastern ridge commandery." Historical figures such as Su Shi and Wen Tianxiang have left their footprints here.

惠州西湖

West Lake of Huizhou

惠州西湖是国家 5A 级旅游景区，与杭州西湖、颍州西湖并称为"中国三大西湖"。湖光山色秀丽，人文景观丰富，苏东坡曾在此留下"一自坡公谪南海，天下不敢小惠州"的千古名句。

West Lake of Huizhou is a national 5A tourist attraction, together with Hangzhou West Lake and Yingzhou West Lake, known as "China's Three West Lakes." It features beautiful lake and mountain scenery and rich cultural landscapes. Su Shi once left the famous verse here: "Since Master Su was exiled to Nanhai, no one dares to underestimate Huizhou."

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于初中英语阅读与写作课，可作为景点介绍写作模板，让学生学习用英语描述家乡美景。

东坡祠

Dongpo Memorial Temple

东坡祠位于惠州市区，是为纪念北宋文学家苏轼而建的祠堂。苏轼于绍圣元年被贬惠州，在此居住三年零九个月，留下了众多诗词和文化遗产。

Dongpo Memorial Temple is located in Huizhou urban area, built in memory of Su Shi, a literary master of the Northern Song Dynasty. Su Shi was exiled to Huizhou in 1094 and lived here for three years and nine months, leaving numerous poems and cultural heritage.

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于高中英语文化课，可作为历史人物专题素材，结合东坡诗词英译进行跨文化教学。

巽寮湾

Xunliao Bay

巽寮湾位于惠东县，是粤东地区著名的滨海旅游度假区，拥有优质的海滩和独特的渔家文化。这里的渔民保留着传统的捕鱼方式和渔歌习俗。

Xunliao Bay is located in Huidong County and is a famous coastal tourism resort in eastern Guangdong. It has high-quality beaches and unique fishing culture. The fishermen here retain traditional fishing methods and fishing song customs.

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于初中英语听说课，可作为旅游文化口语素材，让学生学习用英语介绍旅游景点和民俗文化。

红色故事 · Red Stories

惠州红色文化英雄事迹英文讲述

⭐

文化小科普 | Cultural Background

东江纵队历史地位：东江纵队是抗日战争时期中国共产党在华南地区领导的一支重要抗日武装，战斗在东江流域，为华南抗战作出了重大贡献。惠州是东江纵队的重要活动区域，留下了众多革命遗址和英雄事迹。

Historical Status of Dongjiang Column: The Dongjiang Column was an important anti-Japanese armed force led by the Communist Party of China in South China during the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression. It fought in the Dongjiang River basin and made significant contributions to the resistance in South China. Huizhou was an important activity area of the Dongjiang Column, leaving many revolutionary sites and heroic deeds.

东江纵队简介

Introduction to Dongjiang Column

东江纵队成立于 1943 年，是抗日战争时期中国共产党领导的一支重要抗日武装力量。纵队战斗在东江流域，开展游击战争，建立抗日根据地，为华南抗战作出了重大贡献。

The Dongjiang Column was established in 1943 and was an important anti-Japanese armed force led by the Communist Party of China during the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression. The column fought in the Dongjiang River basin, carried out guerrilla warfare, established anti-Japanese base areas, and made significant contributions to the resistance in South China.

曾生将军

General Zeng Sheng

曾生（1910-1995），广东惠阳人，东江纵队的主要创建人和领导人之一。他率领东江纵队在抗日战争中英勇作战，为华南地区的抗日斗争作出了重要贡献。

Zeng Sheng (1910-1995), a native of Huiyang, Guangdong, was one of the main founders and leaders of the Dongjiang Column. He led the Dongjiang Column to fight bravely in the War of Resistance Against Japanese Aggression and made important contributions to the anti-Japanese struggle in South China.

营救文化名人

Rescuing Cultural Celebrities

1942 年，东江纵队在极端困难的条件下，成功营救了数百名被困在香港的文化名人和爱国民主人士，创造了抗战史上的奇迹，被誉为"中国文化史上的伟大壮举"。

In 1942, under extremely difficult conditions, the Dongjiang Column successfully rescued hundreds of cultural celebrities and patriotic democrats trapped in Hong Kong, creating a miracle in the history of the War of Resistance and hailed as "a great feat in the history of Chinese culture."

名人传记 · Famous Biographies

惠州历史文化名人双语传记

📜

文化小科普 | Cultural Background

惠州名人辈出：惠州自古以来就是人杰地灵之地，孕育了众多历史文化名人。从古代的苏轼、文天祥，到近代的廖仲恺、叶挺，再到现代的众多杰出人才，惠州人以勤劳智慧、爱国爱乡著称。

Huizhou's Famous Figures: Huizhou has been a land of remarkable people since ancient times, nurturing numerous famous historical and cultural figures. From ancient figures like Su Shi and Wen Tianxiang to modern figures like Liao Zhongkai and Ye Ting, and many outstanding talents in contemporary times, Huizhou people are known for their diligence, wisdom, patriotism, and love for their hometown.

苏轼

Su Shi (1037-1101)

苏轼，字子瞻，号东坡居士，北宋文学家、书法家、画家。绍圣元年（1094 年）被贬惠州，在惠期间三年零九个月。他关心民生疾苦，推广农业技术，兴办教育，对惠州文化发展影响深远。

Su Shi, courtesy name Zizhan, art name Dongpo Jushi, was a literary master, calligrapher, and painter of the Northern Song Dynasty. In 1094, he was exiled to Huizhou and lived here for three years and nine months. He cared deeply about people's hardships, promoted agricultural techniques, and established schools, profoundly influencing Huizhou's cultural development.

廖仲恺

Liao Zhongkai (1877-1925)

廖仲恺，广东惠阳人，中国近代民主革命家，国民党左派领袖。他早年参加同盟会，追随孙中山先生从事民主革命，为第一次国共合作作出了重要贡献。1925 年在广州遇刺身亡。

Liao Zhongkai (1877-1925), a native of Huiyang, Guangdong, was a modern Chinese democratic revolutionary and leader of the left wing of the Kuomintang. He joined the Tongmenghui early in life and followed Dr. Sun Yat-sen in the democratic revolution, making important contributions to the first KMT-CCP cooperation. He was assassinated in Guangzhou in 1925.

叶挺

Ye Ting (1896-1946)

叶挺，广东惠阳人，中国人民解放军的创建人和领导人之一。他早年参加辛亥革命，后加入中国共产党，参与领导南昌起义、广州起义等重大历史事件。1946 年因飞机失事遇难。

Ye Ting (1896-1946), a native of Huiyang, Guangdong, was one of the founders and leaders of the Chinese People's Liberation Army. He joined the Revolution of 1911 early in life, later joined the Communist Party of China, and participated in leading major historical events such as the Nanchang Uprising and Guangzhou Uprising. He died in an air crash in 1946.

湾区文化融合 · Bay Area Cultural Integration

粤港澳大湾区文化交流与融合

🌉

文化小科普 | Cultural Background

粤港澳大湾区：粤港澳大湾区包括香港特别行政区、澳门特别行政区和广东省的广州、深圳、珠海、佛山、惠州、东莞、中山、江门、肇庆等 9 市，是国家重大发展战略。惠州作为大湾区重要节点城市，在文化交流、产业合作等方面发挥着重要作用。

Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area: The Greater Bay Area includes the Hong Kong and Macao Special Administrative Regions and nine cities in Guangdong Province: Guangzhou, Shenzhen, Zhuhai, Foshan, Huizhou, Dongguan, Zhongshan, Jiangmen, and Zhaoqing. It is a major national development strategy. As an important node city in the Greater Bay Area, Huizhou plays an important role in cultural exchange and industrial cooperation.

湾区文化交流

Bay Area Cultural Exchange

惠州积极参与粤港澳大湾区文化交流活动，通过举办文化节、艺术展览、学术论坛等形式，促进湾区城市间的文化互鉴，推动岭南文化、客家文化、港澳文化的融合发展。

Huizhou actively participates in cultural exchange activities of the Guangdong-Hong Kong-Macao Greater Bay Area. Through hosting cultural festivals, art exhibitions, academic forums, and other forms, it promotes cultural mutual learning among Bay Area cities and advances the integrated development of Lingnan culture, Hakka culture, and Hong Kong-Macao culture.

客家文化传承

Inheritance of Hakka Culture

惠州是客家文化的重要发祥地之一，拥有丰富的客家文化资源。在湾区建设背景下，惠州加强客家文化保护与传承，推动客家围屋、客家山歌、客家饮食文化等走向湾区、走向世界。

Huizhou is one of the important birthplaces of Hakka culture and has rich Hakka cultural resources. In the context of the Bay Area construction, Huizhou strengthens the protection and inheritance of Hakka culture and promotes Hakka walled villages, Hakka mountain songs, Hakka cuisine, and other cultural elements to the Bay Area and the world.

创新合作发展

Innovation and Cooperative Development

惠州依托大湾区平台，加强与港澳在科技创新、教育合作、人才培养等方面的合作。通过共建实验室、联合培养人才、开展学术交流等方式，推动湾区教育文化协同发展。

Relying on the Greater Bay Area platform, Huizhou strengthens cooperation with Hong Kong and Macao in technological innovation, educational cooperation, talent training, and other aspects. Through building joint laboratories, jointly training talents, and conducting academic exchanges, it promotes the coordinated development of education and culture in the Bay Area.
