林则徐 · Lin Zexu (1785-1850)

📖 事迹介绍 | Story

中文：林则徐，福建侯官人，清朝政治家、思想家和诗人。道光十八年（1838 年），他被任命为钦差大臣，前往广东查禁鸦片。他在虎门海滩当众销毁了英商交出的 2 万多箱鸦片，显示了中华民族反抗外来侵略的坚强意志。这一壮举成为第一次鸦片战争的导火索，也开启了中国近代史的序幕。

English: Lin Zexu, a native of Houguan, Fujian, was a statesman, thinker, and poet of the Qing Dynasty. In 1838, he was appointed Imperial Commissioner and sent to Guangdong to ban opium. He publicly destroyed over 20,000 chests of opium surrendered by British merchants on Humen Beach, demonstrating the Chinese nation's strong will to resist foreign aggression. This feat became the trigger for the First Opium War and opened the prologue of modern Chinese history.

⭐ 核心精神 | Core Spirit

  • 爱国主义精神：以国家利益为重，不顾个人安危，坚决禁烟
  • 民族气节：面对列强压力，毫不退缩，维护国家尊严
  • 改革精神：主张学习西方先进技术，"师夷长技以制夷"
  • Patriotism: Prioritizing national interests over personal safety, resolutely banning opium
  • National Integrity: Facing pressure from powers without flinching, safeguarding national dignity
  • Reform Spirit: Advocating learning from Western advanced technology to counter the West

💡 教学拓展问题 | Discussion Questions

Reading Comprehension (阅读理解):

1. What was Lin Zexu's major achievement in Guangdong? (林则徐在广东的主要成就是什么？)

2. Why is the destruction of opium at Humen considered a significant historical event? (为什么虎门销烟被认为是一个重要的历史事件？)

Critical Thinking (思辨讨论):

3. How do you understand Lin Zexu's spirit of "learning from the West to counter the West"? In today's context, what can we learn from this? (你如何理解林则徐"师夷长技以制夷"的精神？在当今时代，我们可以从中学习什么？)

Writing Task (写作任务):

4. Write a short essay (150-200 words) titled "The Patriotism of Lin Zexu and Its Contemporary Value". (写一篇 150-200 词的短文，题目为"林则徐的爱国主义精神及其当代价值")

📚 教学提示： 本素材适用于高中英语阅读与写作课，可作为历史人物专题素材和爱国主义教育素材。建议结合鸦片战争历史背景进行跨学科教学，培养学生的家国情怀和历史责任感。
